How to use?

Double-click to highlight
When you double-click a word on the page, it will be highlighted with a unique background color. If the word has already been highlighted, it will retain its color.
Triple-click to change the color
If you triple-click a highlighted word, its color will change to a new randomly generated background color.
Right-click to clear all highlights
If you right-click anywhere on the page, all highlights will be removed, and the word color map will be reset.
